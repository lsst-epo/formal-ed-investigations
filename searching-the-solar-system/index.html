Searching the Solar System

Introduction

One hundred years ago, we had a fairly simple model of the components our solar system: some planets and moons, comets, and asteroids between Mars and Jupiter. Advances in technology have provided a more powerful ability to detect objects that are smaller and farther from the Sun, so our model of the solar system today is more complete.
